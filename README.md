NOTE: For those who simply want a colorful digital clock with many features, the Pico-RGB-Matrix in another of my GitHub repositories may be what you are looking for.<br/><br/>
However...<br/><br/>
For those who want to go one step further and begin the deployment of a whole Smart Home ecosystem, the Pico-SLASH-Display is probably a good place to start. 
Like the Pico-RGB-Matrix, it is based on the same Waveshare’s electronic device (the « Pico-RGB-Matrix ») but the Firmware has been designed with the idea to communicate with other devices on the same Smart Home ecosystem.<br/>

To get more details, simply download the User Guide in the repository. You'll get pages of information.<br/>
